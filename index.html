Our Story

Tooba was founded with a vision to revive the intricate art of Pakistani textiles. Based in the cultural hubs of Pakistan, we collaborate with local artisans to bring you designs that blend centuries-old craftsmanship with modern silhouettes.


Every thread in a Tooba garment tells a story of heritage, passion, and elegance. We believe that fashion is not just what you wear, but how you carry your culture.
